About us

We Never Compromise With Quality
Also Providing Reasonable Price!

Arun Furniture Mart is the premium brand for furniture in Kerala located in Cherpunkal. We are the traditional furniture dealers in Kottayam and all over Kerala dealing with standard range of furniture sets and interior items. We cater our customers for all their furniture needs for their home or office at value prices, keeping luxury within reach of design conscious. From tables and chairs to storage, our modern furniture collections allow you to create a home that matches your lifestyle.
